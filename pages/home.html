Featured Content

Optimal Particle Identification

A paper I wrote with a colleague at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab, in which we derive a heuristic that can be used to distinguish particle types from the energy-loss reaouts they leave in the ATLAS detector at CERN.

Shor's Algorithm Talk

A talk I gave at the Bay Area Quantum Computing Meetup in August of 2017, describing the math behind Shor's Algorithm, the impact it will have on modern cryptosystems, and demonstrating a toy example that can be run on today's hardware.

Exclusive Quark and Gluon Jet Properties

A talk I gave at the ATLAS annual meeting at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab in January of 2017. I presented a regression model I designed to estimate three-dimensional distributions of particle jets measured at CERN.
